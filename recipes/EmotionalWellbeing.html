Emotional wellbeing!

Welcome to your happy little recipe for emotional wellbeing. Just like creating a masterpiece, we'll blend together the perfect ingredients to make your day a little brighter.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup of smiles
  • 2 tablespoons of laughter
  • 1 teaspoon of gratitude
  • A dash of kindness
  • A sprinkle of positivity

    • Optional
  • 1 hug (for extra warmth)

Steps

  1. Start with a blank canvas and a deep breath. Appreciate the beauty around you.
  2. Add a generous cup of smiles. Remember, there are no mistakes, only happy little accidents.
  3. Blend in two tablespoons of laughter. Let it bubble up naturally, like a stream in a forest.
  4. Mix in a teaspoon of gratitude. Reflect on the little things that bring you joy.
  5. Gently fold in a dash of kindness. Spread it to those around you, and watch it come back.
  6. Sprinkle with positivity. Even a little can go a long way in brightening your day.
