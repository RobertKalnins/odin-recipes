Easy Temaki

Easy Hand Rolled Sushi/Temaki

Temaki is hand rolled sushi and it's a great easy snack to make

This is a very accessible recipie and a really fun hand food especially for teaching kids about different types of foods

Don't worry about making it look like the picture, the best thing about this recipie is it's very accessible and fun

Ingredients

  • Nori sheets - you can buy temaki sheets or just cut regular sheets in half
  • Cooked Sticky Rice - best prepared as Sushi rice
  • Various ingredients chopped lengthways
  • e.g. Cucumbers, carrots, avocado, salmon, chicken, tofu, or anything else you can think of
  • Sauces - Soy sauce, Kewpie mayonnaise, Chimichurri, Peanut sauce, Curry sauce, Harissa, Worcestershire, try anything!
  • Garnishes - again, try anything! - pickled ginger is great, I like slivers of fresh garlic, sundried tomatoes, even sliced pickled onions
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare your rice - any sushi rice recipie from allrecipes.com will do. You can also use microwaved rice packets as long as the rice comes out reasonably sticky
  2. Prepare a platter of ingredients - you don't have to stick to standard sushi ingredients, it's fun to mix and match different textures and flavours
  3. Grab a sheet of nori in your hand and start with speading rice on the paper - it should be dry and crinkly - don't wet it first, the rice will make it malleable.
  4. Now layer any other ingredients on and add your sauces and garnishes
  5. Roll or fold it up

    6. Optional
  6. If you're just making a quick snack for yourself you can cut the sheets into 4 squares, add some sauces and garnishes to rice, then with a spoon make small rice balls and grab it with the nori. With a little practice this is a very easy technique to do without creating mess
