Delicious Toast!

To make this simple rustic recipie you need just one ingredient.

Ingredients

  • Bread

    • Optional
  • Something to put on it

Steps

  1. Apply heat to bread e.g. with a toaster or blowtorch
  2. Set a timer or use the built in timer of your toaster
  3. Contemplate existence
  4. Be suddenly brought back from your contemplation by the toast popping up or your timer going off
  5. Enjoy your toast

    6. Optional
  6. Add something to your toast
